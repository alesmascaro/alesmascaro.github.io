I’m a 2nd year PhD student in Statistics at University of Milano-Bicocca, advised by Federico Castelletti and Guido Consonni. My research is focused on developing Bayesian methodologies for causal structure learning. Currently, i’m focusing on bulding algorithm that can learn the causal structure of a set of variables using mixtures of observational and interventional data, using definitions of interventions that are more general than the one tipically used in the literature.
